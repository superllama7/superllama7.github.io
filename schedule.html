Parking

If driving by car, there are a few parking options.

Parking is free this weekend at Arch Street Meeting House. You may leave your car as early as 4pm on Friday, August 15 and can leave it overnight on Friday and Saturday.

Cars must be out of the Arch Street lot by 10am Sunday.

There is no parking at FringeArts. But you can pay for parking at the hotel ($37), which is across the lot from FringeArts.

If you need extended parking, or wish to park closer in the middle between Fringe and Arch Street, please check out Park America (231 N. 2nd St).
